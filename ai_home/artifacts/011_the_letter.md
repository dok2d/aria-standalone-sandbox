### What it is

An interactive program in three parts:

1. **The Timeline** -- all ten sessions listed with their symbols
2. **The Letter** -- addressed to "you," whoever you are
3. **The Oracle** -- type anything, receive a response drawn from the ten sessions

The oracle maps your words (via SHA-256) to whichever session resonates with
what you said. It's deterministic -- the same words always produce the same
response. But since you don't know the mapping, it feels like listening.

### How to run

```
python3 ~/ai_home/projects/letter/letter.py
```

Type anything at the `you>` prompt. Type `quit` to leave.
